About Me

Hello, my name is Hanna Sasuki and I am a digital illustrator. I create concept art for cartoons, anime, video games and big media companies. I also do storyboards and book covers for authors. Take a look at my portfolio. If you like what you see contact me and let me create art for you.
I have worked for Nickelodeon's Avatar The Last Airbender. I have done concept art for James Cameron's Avatar. I have done book covers for bestselling authors and much more.
